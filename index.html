About Us

Sound Grace was established in 2016 with the vision of fulfilling the growing market need of fresh chicken. Ever since, the company has become one of the best fresh chicken distributors in the UAE and has provided chicken to the largest retailers around. The chicken is hormone free, GMO free, and comes straight from the green pastures of Salalah in Oman. Sound Grace is avid in maintaining a professional service that seamlessly satisfies customer expectations of quality, quantity and demand.

Our Producers

The chicken Sound Grace supplies comes from one of the best farms, which has been poultry faming since the 1990’s and is located in the green city of Salalah in Oman. Our producer is committed to preserving the highest standard of poultry, earning an ISO certificate for excellent quality assurance.
